Trade

Coming Soon!

Piratecoin will be available to trade on Pangolin Swap

How To Buy:

  1. Install MetaMask
  2. Buy AVAX on an exchange (CEX) like Coinbase or Binance
  3. Send AVAX to your C-Chain MetaMask wallet
  4. Buy Piratecoin

Supply Liquidity

Get Paid to Supply Liquidity!

When you supply liquidity to Pangolin Swap, you help make trading Piratecoin possible. To do this, you must add AVAX or Piratecoin to the liquidy pool. Traders will pay fees to trade the assets held in this pool, and you will collect these fees proportional to your ownership of the pool.
